About Us

The Student Council is a group of students who make up the student body. Their main responsibility is to organize fun events for the school and make it more enjoyable. We are a the student council of our school. We consist of around 30 students, two from each class. Our job is to organize fun events for our school through the school year to make it more enjoyable. Examples of events organized by us include picnics and parties. The Student Council is made up of members from each class, so there are always two people from each class on this committee. They meet once in a while to discuss how they can make things better at school and have fun activities for everyone to enjoy.


Student council is the organization that keeps our school's students in check. We are responsible for organizing many different events for our school, including picnics and parties. We also help out in other ways, such as organizing fundraisers for our school or helping out with sports teams. We are a fun group of people who enjoy being around each other and making new friends. We love to have fun together, which is why we always try to make sure that everyone has a good time when they attend one of our parties or events!
